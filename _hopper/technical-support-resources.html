Technical support resources


If you're having a problem, you can always ask one of the support resources below.

Simply choose a resource, press the "Go" button, and explain to them what your issue is.

Go →

Official Minecraft Discord

Visit the official Minecraft to get help from the Minecraft community!

Go →

Community IRC support

IRC, is a way for people to chat and hold conversations over the internet, no registrations required. For support with Minecraft;

You can join the #minecrafthelp channel, by going pressing the 'GO' button to your right, please be sure to let them know about the problem you have, and an Mojira issue ID, if you have one!

Go →

Minecraft Forum

The Minecraft Forum are an active community of Minecraft players who create topics and reply to each others' post.

If you have a question about something, you can search through the archive of topics, or register for an account if you have not already and create your own topic.

Go →

PlanetMinecraft

PlanetMinecraft also has a support forum in which you can create, and reply to topics much like the official Minecraft Forums.

To use the service, simply register and create a topic explaining your problem.
